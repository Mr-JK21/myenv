1. Account Registering

Provide all the necessary details for registering in website.

  • Username (required)
  • Password(required)
  • Confirm Password(required)
  • Mobile phone numbers
  • Email address (required)
  • City
  • Security Question
  • Answer
  • Role(Seller / Buyer)

2. Check Product

Check Product from home page or go for details and click on order button.

3. Placing Order

Now the order is initiated but it is in pending state that means the seller has not accepted your order. Here the order status will be displayed for all your order.

4. Order Status

As the status become blue that means Ready status than you can go and pick up your order.

5. Pick up

Go to that store and tell your username and orderid thean the seller will confirm and hand your items to you after payment.
